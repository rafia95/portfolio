The Harry Potter Memory Game

How to Play:

The rules are simple!

Click or press any alphabet from A to K, you will see a Harry Potter character, Choose another picture, if the hidden character matches the previous character you can see the super awesome picture in the back!! Keep on choosing the pictures to uncover the whole background picture!!


Go! Find your favourite Harry Potter character! You might find Harry, Ron or, maybe, you-know-who!!
